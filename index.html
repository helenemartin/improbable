Improbable- Provisional title for a responsive video webpage

Video webpage for an Art show in Copenhagen "Gaming,Gaming" and part of a display at Rotterdam Film Festival 2014.

View the Project on GitHub helenemartin/improbable

For Gaming Gaming, I have been asked to respond to Rachel Cattle's text above.

"That night in a Birmingham basement the emotional force of her voice was held in constant tension with a palpable anxiety in her performance, which gave the music a nerve-shredding high-wire tension; by the end of the evening, the audience... seemed to be collectively willing her on to her next improbable feat of melody; an encore seemed almost an unfair ordeal to impose on her."

The gallery visitor will be able to view the video from their smart phone or tablets by scanning a QR sign on the wall.
